Rijksmuseum Thief Game
Instructions for the game

For this game, the objective is to steal the paintings of the Rijksmuseum and not to get caught. If you are caught it's game over. Hover over the painting to show the name of the painting, if you are willing to steal the painting. You can also click on the painting to see the full painting and additional information about the painting. Select the name of the painting on the input to the left. Press "Steal" if you want to steal the selected paintings and they will appear on the list to the right. When you want to leave the museum press "Leave the museum". You can get caught stealing or by leaving the museum. The game will be over and will reset and you can start stealing again.
