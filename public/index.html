Thievery


Instructions for the game

For this game, the objective is to steal a lot of paintings of the Rijksmuseum and not to get caught.
If you are caught it's game over, if you aren't caught you win. Hover over the painting to show the name of the painting.
You can also click on the painting to see the full painting and additional information, which you might need, about the painting.
